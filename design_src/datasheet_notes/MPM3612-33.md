# MPM3612GLQ-33 (3.3V Power Module)

Source: `design_src/datasheet/MPM3612GLQ-33.pdf`

## Firmware-Relevant Pins
- `EN`:
  - Digital enable; apply logic high to enable, pull low to disable.
  - Do not float EN (datasheet explicitly notes not to float).
  - Datasheet includes EN rising threshold range and hysteresis.
- `PG`:
  - Open-drain power-good output.
  - Datasheet behavior summary:
    - After VOUT is in regulation, there is a deglitch delay and then `PG pulls high` (via external pull-up, since PG is open drain).
    - `PG pulls low` when VOUT drops below UV threshold or during OV condition, and immediately when UVLO/OTP occurs or EN goes low.
    - Therefore, with an external pull-up, `PG = HIGH` indicates "power good", `PG = LOW` indicates "not good".

## Protections / Behaviors (high level)
- UVLO, OVP, OCP, thermal shutdown.
- Output discharge behavior when disabled via EN is described in datasheet.
